What is SERP?

When you search anything on Google, the page you see is called a SERP (Search Engine Results Page). It displays results based on your query and helps you find the most relevant information quickly.

Core SERP Elements:

  • 1
    Organic Results: The non-paid websites Google trusts based on relevance.
  • 2
    Paid Ads: Results with the "Sponsored" label (Google Ads).
  • 3
    Local Map Listings: Crucial for Local SEO in Pune.
  • 4
    Featured Snippets: Direct answers shown at the top.

How Search Engines Work

Search engines follow a structured four-stage process to deliver results:

1. Crawling

Search bots scan websites and discover new/updated content.

2. Indexing

Pages are stored in a massive database for quick retrieval.

3. Processing

Algorithms analyze content relevance and intent.

4. Ranking

The best results are displayed based on 200+ authority factors.
